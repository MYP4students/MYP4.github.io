Who We Are

In today's world, almost every decision we make has an impact on the environment. But understanding which choices are most important for protecting the planet can be overwhelming. That's why we, the students studying digital design in MYP 4, have come together with a mission. We want to create a website that shines a light on the environmental issues we face and offers practical solutions to address them.
